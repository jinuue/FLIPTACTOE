Sample Message

FLIP TAC TOE!

How To Play:

Players take turns flipping cards.

Symbols are randomly placed on the cards at the start of the game.

Click on a card to flip and reveal its symbol. Once revealed, the symbol stays visible.

If you accidentally reveal the opponent's symbol, you cannot undo the move, and the game will continue.

Win by lining up three of the same symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.

If all cards are revealed and no one has three in a line, the game is a draw.
